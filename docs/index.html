Brief Introduction

Provide a bit more context: motivation, system overview, and the main contributions or features of Agri-Cruiser.

  • A modular over-the-row chassis with adjustable track width and high clearance.
  • Large, agile platform with payload capacity; differential drive enabling a 0.79 m pivot-turn radius.
  • Precision sprayer integration for herbicide application and data collection.
  • Field validation showing reduced weed density and low crop damage across terrains and crops.
